⚙️ How It Works

1

Signal Processing

Raw abdominal ECG → bandpass filter (3‑45 Hz) → maternal ECG cancellation → R‑peak detection (CNN, 96.8% accuracy) → RR intervals

2

Feature Extraction

RR intervals → 12 HRV features across time, frequency, and nonlinear domains → 21,600+ data points from 1,800+ recordings

3

AI Modeling

Unsupervised clustering (K‑means, hierarchical, DBSCAN) → discovers developmental patterns without predefined labels

📊 12 HRV Features We Extract

⏱️

Time Domain

Mean RR, SDNN, RMSSD, pNN50

RMSSD = Vagal tone – increases with gestational age
SDNN = Total autonomic variability

📡

Frequency Domain

LF Power, HF Power, LF/HF Ratio, Total Power

LF/HF ratio decreases with gestational age → shift toward parasympathetic dominance

🌀

Nonlinear

Sample Entropy, Poincaré SD1, Poincaré SD2, SD1/SD2 Ratio

Sample Entropy peaks at 32 weeks – reflects neural complexity
