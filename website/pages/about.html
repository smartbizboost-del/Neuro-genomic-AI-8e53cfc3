Understanding fetal development through AI and heart rate signals

Clinical-grade analytics, research-ready transparency, and equitable access to prenatal intelligence.

Our Mission

To empower clinicians, researchers, and families with ethical AI that reveals the hidden story of fetal nervous system development — transforming prenatal care from reactive monitoring to proactive understanding.

We believe that every heartbeat contains information. By unlocking the patterns within heart rate variability, we can detect developmental trajectories, identify potential concerns earlier, and ultimately improve outcomes for mothers and babies worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for AI-powered fetal nervous system assessment — deployed in every major hospital, research institution, and medical device platform across the world.

We envision a future where no parent has to wonder if their baby's nervous system is developing normally, because the data will be clear, transparent, and actionable.

What Makes Us Unique

We're not just another AI health startup. Here's what sets Neuro-Genomic AI apart.

🧠

Unsupervised Discovery, Not Just Prediction

Most AI models are supervised — they learn to predict what you already know. Our unsupervised approach discovers patterns no one has seen before, revealing novel developmental trajectories.

The benefit: Discover what you didn't know to look for.
🛡️

Ethical AI Built Into Every Layer

We don't treat ethics as a compliance checkbox. Uncertainty visualization, confidence intervals, and transparent limitations are core features — not afterthoughts.

The benefit: Trust what the AI tells you. Know when it's uncertain.
📈

Longitudinal Tracking, Not Snapshots

Other tools give you a point-in-time risk score. We track individual developmental trajectories across gestation — showing how each fetus is progressing over time.

The benefit: See development as a journey, not just a moment.
☁️

Software-Only, Scalable by Design

No hardware costs. No manufacturing delays. Our cloud-native platform deploys instantly and scales to thousands of hospitals.

The benefit: Lower costs, faster deployment, global reach.
