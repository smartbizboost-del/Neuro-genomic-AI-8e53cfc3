1. Purpose and Nature of the Service

The Neuro-Genomic AI is a research and development tool designed to analyze fetal ECG signals and provide heart rate variability (HRV), PRSA, and related insights for fetal development monitoring, with a focus on risks such as intrauterine growth restriction (IUGR) and high-risk pregnancies.
