Frequently Asked Questions

What inputs does the system support?

Fetal ECG data from CSV, EDF, or PhysioNet collection formats. Compatible with existing abdominal ECG capture devices.

How does the system ensure signal quality?

Built-in SQA evaluates each recording, highlights noisy channels, and prevents analysis if quality is insufficient.

Can this integrate with national systems?

Yes. We support FHIR R4 export and KenyaEMR-ready payloads, enabling surveillance and health information exchange.

How low is the per-test cost?

The platform is software-first, with marginal test cost designed below $0.10 using existing ECG hardware and no consumables.
