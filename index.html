About Us

Understanding development by listening to the earliest signals of life.

Neuro-Genomic AI analyzes fetal heart rate variability using unsupervised machine learning to discover developmental patterns of the autonomic nervous system — the first window into functional brain development before birth.

With 1,800+ recordings analyzed, 12 HRV features extracted, and 329 automated tests passing, our platform is validated, reliable, and ready for research and clinical applications.
